Lucas Torben Meyer

Early Life

Lucas Torben Meyer was born on August 7th, 557 to Jason Meyer and Leah Meyer. He was born fragile with only 1 health in total so he was given a pet/protector named Mint when he was 2 years old. At age 5 his father made a deal with a grader of space, time, and destruction that woud require the grader to protect lucas but at the cost of losing his wife who he had died ten days after the deal. Lucas was raised inside of their chocolates castle home and was never allowed outside due to fear of losing lucas as well. When lucas was 9 and 1/2 he was able to convice mint to take him somewhere far away which caused them to crash land on the long forgotten and ruined middle planet. During the crash he had a near death experience where he encountered the being of time, space, and destruciton who told him about who they are where during this Lucas had named the being Leah. Once Lucas gained conciousness mint and him wondered the planet searching for somewhere to stay when he encountered a home that had been passed down throughout his family which he had made his permanent home.
